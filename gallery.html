About Me



Hi! My name is Marissa. I grew up in Southwest Colorado in the beautiful Rocky Mountains and I'm a proud member of the Navajo Nation. I didn't discover my passion for Computer Science until my sophomore year in college. I took one course and now I've completed my Bachelor's degree and MS in Computer Science from Dartmouth College in 2014 and 2015 respectively. In my last year I worked as a senior designer and developer at Dartmouth's Digital Arts and Innovation Lab (DALI). There I enjoyed working on both the design and development aspects of web, iOS, and product development projects from beginning to end. Since then, I've discovered my passion for traveling by visiting Japan, Vietnam, and Cambodia and I like to spend my free time I enjoy reading, hiking in the mountains, and chilling with my cat.
