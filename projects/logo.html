Personal Logo Design

Design: Adobe Illustrator


Inspirations

This is a showcase of my personal logo design process. I wanted my logo to represent some important aspects of my life, so I drew my inspiration from my favorite animal (red pandas), the landscape that always makes me feel most at home (mountains), and a symbol of my Navajo heritage (geometric rug-designs).


Thumbnail Sketches

With these inspirations in mind, I drew up some thumbnail sketches.


Initial Designs

From these sketches, I played with a few themes and made these intial designs.


Iterations

Once I had an idea I liked, I decided to iterate it to death, varying the colors, the text, and the background


Final Logo

After iterating, I developed this, my final logo!
